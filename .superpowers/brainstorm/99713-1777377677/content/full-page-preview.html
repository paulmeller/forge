Why open source

Stripe runs 1,300 autonomous PRs per week with an internal tool their engineers built. That capability shouldn't require a $200/mo subscription and a proprietary black box.

Forge is MIT-licensed fleet orchestration. The Ledger is in your database. The budget controls are in your config. The agent adapter is a single interface you can swap, extend, or replace.
