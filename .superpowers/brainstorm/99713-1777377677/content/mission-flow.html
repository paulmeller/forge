How a Mission runs

1
Plan

You describe the change. Forge finds the repos, generates tasks, previews them for your approval.

2
Dispatch

Forge launches parallel agent sessions — one per repo, concurrency-controlled, budget-gated.

3
Gate

Each task opens a PR, waits for CI, retries with feedback on failure. Up to 3 attempts.

✓
Merge

CI passes, policy allows — auto-merge. Everything recorded in the Ledger.
